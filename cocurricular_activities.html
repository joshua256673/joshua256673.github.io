Wellstar Bright Secondary School in Mukono, Uganda, offers a variety of co-curricular activities designed to complement academic learning through sports, creative arts, and entrepreneurship.
The school is a Seventh-day Adventist-based institution that schedules these activities after standard lesson hours.
 Co-Curricular Activities
  • Sports & Athletics:
  • Students participate in various sports, including swimming and general field athletics.
  • Creative Arts & Music
  • The student's talents are promoted  through show casing their talents to the school .

 

  • Entrepreneurship Clubs:
  • A notable feature is the Muise Enterprises Business Club (MEBC), which focuses on practical skills like manufacturing and selling craft shoes and sandals.
  • Social Events:
  •  The school hosts traditional milestones such as prom parties.
Facilities and Religious Foundation
As a Seventh-day Adventist school, Wellstar Bright integrates spiritual growth into its daily operations and facilities. The school operates as a private mixed day and boarding school located on the Mukono-Kayunga Road.
